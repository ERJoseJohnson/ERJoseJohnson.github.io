Weekly Bible study

And let us consider how to stir up one another to love and good works, not neglecting to meet together, as is the habit of some, but encouraging one another, and all the more as you see the Day drawing near.
Hebrews 10:24-25 (ESV)
The Bible is the living Word of God, and during our weekly Bible studies, we diligently study the Bible and help each other apply what we have learnt in our daily lives.

Details

We meet every Tuesday evening from 7.30pm-9.30pm. We also meet for dinner before each session at 6.30pm.
