Evangelism

How then will they call on him in whom they have not believed? And how are they to believe in him of whom they have never heard? And how are they to hear without someone preaching?
Romans 10:14 (ESV)
The gospel of Jesus Christ saves, and we boldly proclaim this gospel on campus by holding events that allow our friends on campus to question Christianity and the gospel.

Some of our Events

Lunchtime Talks are a series of short talks that hope to address specific questions that people might have about the gospel. At the end of this talk, attendees are given a chance to ask questions and clarify their understanding on the matter.

Dial-a-Dessert is a seasonal event that happens on Easter and Christmas. Along with a delicious dessert, participants are given an answer to a question about the occasion.

Grill-a-Pastor is a no-holds-barred QnA session where seekers come with their questions, misgivings and doubts about Christianity and we do our best to provide biblically faithful answers.

Partner-Prayer-Pizza is a regular event where we share the gospel with our fellow schoolmates, pray for one another... and also have pizza.

Walk Ups is a regular event where we share the gospel with our fellow schoolmates and get to know more about what they think about the world's problems.
