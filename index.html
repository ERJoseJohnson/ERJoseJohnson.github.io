Why join us?

We think there’s no better way for you to spend your time
than joining us at CF.

  • Inclusive

    Open to Christians, newcomers and all others alike.

  • Biblical

    We spend time listening to God, by reading his word, the bible, and thinking about what it looks like to live it out.

  • Community

    As a community of people who follow Jesus, we seek to try and help each other live for Jesus on campus here, at SUTD.

  • Evangelism

    We put on events, to help answer people’s questions about Jesus, and run courses to give people opportunity to investigate the claims of Jesus for themselves.
