What is Just Looking?

Jesus forms the cornerstone of the Christian faith. But who is Jesus? What has he come to do? What does he say about us? Join us as we help you find the answers to these questions in eyewitness accounts from the Bible. Just Looking spans 6 sessions as we dive into the Gospel according to John.

Just Looking is a good opportunity to know more about Jesus in a chill setting and a fantastic opportunity to bring your friends along too!

Details

We meet every Wednesday from 2pm - 3.30pm at the Campus Center/Canteen.
Coffee, tea and snacks will be provided!
