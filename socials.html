Socials, Retreats and Camps

Socials are scattered throughout the term and are a time of fun and bonding, where you get to connect with your friends at CF through various activities!
Retreats usually occur at the start of a new year, where we take some time off as a fellowship to think hard about a specific book of the Bible and dig deep to figure out the intent and purpose of the book.
Camps are usually held around the time of the May-August term, where we think about a specific theme that runs throughout the Bible and work out how it impacts our lives and our walk.
Feel free to take a look through all the fun times we had over the past years!
