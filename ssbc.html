Singapore Student's Bible Conference

And let us consider how to stir up one another to love and good works, not neglecting to meet together, as is the habit of some, but encouraging one another, and all the more as you see the Day drawing near.
Hebrews 10:24-25 (ESV)
Twice a year, students from 8 campus ministries gather at SMU to hear God's Word and worship together.

Details

SSBC occurs twice a year. Once in Summer and once in Winter.

Summer SSBC
Summer SSBC consists of 6 weeks of talks about a book in the Bible, centered around a specific theme. You can view some examples of previous Summer SSBC schedules below.

Winter SSBC
Winter SSBC consists of a 4 day camp, filled with workshops, talks, bible studies and oh so many games. It is a fantastic time to worship with your peers from other Singaporean schools! You can view a sample Winter SSBC schedule below!
